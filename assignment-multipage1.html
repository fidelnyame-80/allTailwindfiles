We offer a wide variety of planning and transactional services for your business and your personal life. We believe in the value of preparedness, both in business and in life, and we help our clients plan and prepare for the future so that they are well taken care of.

Business

We work with businesses and business owners in every stage of the cycle, from formation, to ongoing operation and growth of the company, preparation of contracts, protection of intellectual property, and the sale or closing of a business.

Real Estate

We work with businesses and individuals on a wide variety of real estate matters, including lease review and negotiattion, preparation of deeds and purchase and sale agreements, and boundary line and easement issues.

Estates

We help people to protect their assets and their families through comprehensive estate planning services, including the preparation of Wills, Trusts, Power of Attorney, and Healthcare Directives. We also help with the administration of estates through Probate.
